A classic chicken dish features tender, juicy meat marinated with spices and seared to lock in flavor.
Aromatic ingredients like garlic, ginger, and fresh herbs build depth while a balance of acid and fat brightens the sauce.
Slow braising yields fall-off-the-bone texture, while high-heat roasting gives crisp, golden skin.
Serve with steamed rice, crusty bread, or roasted vegetables to soak up the sauce and complete the meal.
Finish with fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon for brightness and a final flavor lift.
